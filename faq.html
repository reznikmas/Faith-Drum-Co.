Frequently Asked Questions

If your question isn't answered here, please feel free to contact us by sending us a message on our contact page.

Q: If no one is at the shop and I cannot reach anyone on the phone, may I still buy a barrel?

A: Absolutely! We trust people enough to be honest. Simply grab whatever barrel you need, and put the money in the drawer of the desk that is directly to the left when you walk inside of the shop.

Q: How much does a barrel cost?

A: That depends on what kind of barrel you are looking for. If you just want something to burn trash in, we have something to suit you for just $10.

Q: I want to make a grill out of a barrel. How do I prepare the barrel to make it safe for preparing food?

A: All you need to do is make an extremely hot fire inside of the barrel, which will burn out all of the harmful chemicals, so it can become a grill. We also sell an unlined steel drum that requires no burning.

Q: Are your barrels food-grade?

A: We carry a selection of plastic and steel food-grade drums.

Q: Can you deliver barrels to my house?

A: Absolutely! We are glad to deliver barrels to anywhere within 15 miles of our shop. Just call us and let us know: where you are located, which type of barrel(s) you are interested in, as well as how many barrels you would like.

Q: Are your barrels used? If so, what used to be in them?

A: Yes, most of our barrels are used. Most of our non-food grade drums contained automotive paint or thinner. Our food-grade steel drums contained cooking oil, while our plastic barrels contained lactic acid or soy sauce. All of our food-grade drums are washed thoroughly inside and out using soap and hot water from a high-powered pressure washer when we receive them.
